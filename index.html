Mission Statement

Our mission is to give people an accessible way of learning about safe farming practices and buying from local farms that use those good farming practices

Description

This website was created to help people find produce from healthy and humane raised animals, help support local, family owned farms, and bring awareness to the things that happen on factory farms

Explore our website to find out more and donate to local farms and organizations that support good farming practices.
